Dice

Was thinking of a simple object that couldn't be made with subtractive manafacturing and decided on a dice (subtractive wouldn't be able to reach all the holes). Made it simply by creating a cube and adding a couple small holes then smoothing the edges. Dice Files

Caesar Pencil Holder

Julius Caesar Pencil Holder. Got inspiration for this when I saw a similar design while browsing an office supply website. For the body I used a human file I found off of thingiverse and cropped it a little. I then used one of the tools to create a grid of holes. I filled up the holes on the front side with the brush tool and then smoothed out the sculpture a bit. Most of the holes can hold a pencil but some struggle with it.
